Sara.

I'm Sara Weinand

A 22 year old graphic designer and web enthusiast from Washington (the state). I specialize in the design & development of the web. However, I also enjoy creating work that doesn't belong on the internet. I try to find inspiration in anything and everything in order to craft unique and creative design every time.

I’d like to say I have hobbies, but I’m a student (almost a graduate), so we all know that wouldn’t be the truth. However, when I do have free time, I’m usually immersed in design books while snuggling with my two dogs.

I AM...

A Creative Problem Solver,

When I'm faced with a creative problem, I will do whatever it takes to solve it. Pulling inspiration from this giant world around me, I can figure out new solutions to old problems. Just as finding a solution is important, I believe it is important to display the solution in a fresh and creative way.

Detail Oriented,

I believe in taking special care of the details and paying special attention to each and every pixel in all my work. When all aspects of a piece are taken care of, clients and users feel like the work was created for them. I don't simply make things pretty, I handcraft experiences.

& Passionate!

As a designer, I feel that passion outweighs skill. When you passion for something, the quality will reflect. I'm passionate in my work because I want every client to feel important and every project to be unique enough to stand alone.
